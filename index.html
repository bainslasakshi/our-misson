OUR MISSION







`


To provide good quality modern education, including a strong component of culture, Inculcation of values, awareness of the environment, adventure activities and physical education to the talented children, predominantly from the rural areas, without regard to their family’s socio – economic condition.

School * We have a sprawling campus with excellent facilities and committed faculty. The management of LKPS has a rich experience in running educational institutions at Shri Mahaveer Ji starting from Pre Primary School to Post Graduate College. Not only this, we have expertise in running technical education also catering to the needs of students in different area. Our classrooms and their ventilation and natural light are far superior.

*As we all are aware, one of the problems of our city is absence of playgrounds in schools where we are pioneer and can say it will be very difficult to be copied. The school boasts of a big hall for the purpose of a library which is stocked with approximately 3000 Books.

We also maintain a diverse collection of newspaper, journals, periodicals and magazines for the students. Special, well equipped laboratories are there for different subject for class IX to XII. These are physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics and Computer Labs. Henceforth, we shed light on the Scholastic Area of the academics, which is the basic need of education today. These all will provide parallel quality education to your kid compared to any of the metro of the country.
